🌎💹 **Forex vs Stocks: Which Is Better for Passive Investors?** 

When it comes to passive investing, both forex and stocks offer unique advantages and challenges. Let's break it down! 

**Forex Trading**: 
- The forex market is the largest financial market globally, with daily trading volumes exceeding $6 trillion. 
- Trading occurs 24/5, allowing for flexibility and the potential to capitalize on global economic events.
- With PAMM trading, you can invest passively by relying on experienced traders to manage your funds, maximizing your potential returns without the daily grind.

**Stock Market**: 
- Stocks represent ownership in companies, providing dividends and the potential for long-term capital growth. 
- Markets are typically open for shorter hours (9:30 am - 4 pm), making them more limited for daily trading opportunities.
- However, stock investments often provide more stability and are easier for new investors to understand.

So, which is better for passive investors? It often depends on your risk tolerance, investment goals, and the time you can dedicate to monitoring your investments. 

If you're looking for an easy way to diversify, consider exploring PAMM trading in forex. It's a great way to dip your toes into the market without overwhelming yourself!
