Are you intrigued by the world of forex trading but worried about high barriers to entry? Good news! You can start your forex journey with just $100. Yes, you read that right! 👏

**Here’s how you can kickstart your micro-investing adventure:**

1️⃣ **Choose the Right Broker**: Look for a reputable broker that offers micro accounts. This way, you can trade with smaller lots and manage your risk effectively.

2️⃣ **Educate Yourself**: Knowledge is power. Invest time in learning the basics of forex trading. Utilize demos, online courses, and webinars to sharpen your skills.

3️⃣ **Start Small, Stay Disciplined**: With just $100, focus on developing a sound trading strategy. Stay disciplined, manage your emotions, and stick to your plan. 

4️⃣ **Leverage PAMM Accounts**: Consider partnering with experienced traders through PAMM (Percentage Allocation Management Module) accounts. This allows you to benefit from their expertise while staying passive!

5️⃣ **Monitor Your Progress**: Keep track of your trades and evaluate your performance regularly. Adjust your strategy as needed to grow your investment.
