🌍✨ Are you maximizing your PAMM trading performance by knowing the best forex trading hours? Understanding the market sessions is crucial for anyone looking to achieve passive income through forex trading. 

The forex market operates 24 hours a day, divided into three main sessions: the Asian, European, and North American sessions. Each session has its unique characteristics and activity levels, which can significantly influence your trading outcomes.

🔹 **Asian Session (Tokyo)**: Typically quieter, this session is perfect for those who prefer lower volatility and a more consistent trading environment.

🔹 **European Session (London)**: Known for its high liquidity and volatility, this is often considered one of the best times to trade. Major market moves frequently occur here, creating ample opportunities for PAMM investors.

🔹 **North American Session (New York)**: As it overlaps with the European session, this is another peak time for traders. Major economic news releases often happen during this session, which can lead to sharp market movements.

By aligning your trading strategies with these sessions, you can significantly improve your PAMM performance.
